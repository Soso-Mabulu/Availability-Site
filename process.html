Our Booking Process

1

Browse Services

Explore our range of tutoring services, including programming, database management, and networking.

2

Select Module

Choose the specific module or subject you need assistance with from the dropdown menu.

3

Choose Session Package

Select the number of tutoring sessions you'd like to book: one session, three sessions, or six sessions.

4

Book Session

Fill out the booking form with your name, email, phone number, preferred time, and date.

5

Appoint a Tutor

Our team will appoint a suitable tutor for your module who will then contact you to confirm location, time, and date.

6

Confirmation

Receive a confirmation email with details of your booked session, including tutor information and session logistics.

7

Attend Session

Join the scheduled tutoring session at the specified time via the provided video conferencing link.
